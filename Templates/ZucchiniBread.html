Zucchini Bread

156 Likes 3 Comments 18th May 2019

This recipe is a simple, tasty, and easy to bake bread that is absolutely delicious.
It goes great with a jam of choice or butter! Makes about 24 servings (2 loaves).

While this recipe is meant to be made in bulk, check out our other recipes for our automatic serving size adjuster!
Ingredients
3 Cups All-Purpose Flour 3 Eggs
Salt, 1 tsp Baking Soda, 1 tsp
Ground Cinnamon, 1 tbsp 1 Cup Vegetable Oil
2 1/4 Cups White Sugar Vanilla Extract, 3 tsp
2 Cups Grated Zucchini 1 Cup Chopped Walnuts
Kitchen Needs
Two 8x4 inch Pans Oven
Whisk Bowl
Box Grater Large Knife
Cutting Board Food Processor (optional)


Instructions

1. Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

2. Sift flour, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.

3. Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar together in a large bowl. Add sifted ingredients to the
creamed mixture, and beat well. Stir in zucchini and nuts until well combined.

4. Pour batter into prepared pans.

5. Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes. Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

6. Slice, serve, and enjoy!

Enjoy!
