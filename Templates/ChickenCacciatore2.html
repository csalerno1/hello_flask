Chicken Cacciatore

316 Likes 3 Comments 6th February 2017

This recipe is great for a family meal or Sunday dinner! It is
a classic dish that has an abundance of flavor! Makes about 8 servings.

Check out our automatic serving size adjuster! The recipe can cater to however many guests you are serving. Upon loading, the recipe has a serving size of 8 people.
Ingredients
4 Pounds Cut Up Chicken Salt and Pepper (optional)
2 tbsp of Olive Oil 2 tbsp of Butter
2 Cups of Chopped Onion 2 Celery Ribs, sliced
1 Large Green Pepper, sliced 6 Cups Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
28 oz Canned Diced Tomatoes, drained 8 oz Tomato Sauce
6 oz Tomato Paste 8 oz Dry Red Wine (or Water)
Dried Thyme to Taste Dried Rosemary to Taste
Dried Oregano to Taste Dried Basil to Taste
4 Garlic Cloves, Minced 1 tbsp Sugar
Grated Parmesan Cheese Pasta (Ziti is reccommended)
Kitchen Needs
Large Skillet Pan Stove
Large Pot and Large Plate Spatula
Box Grater Large Knife
Cutting Board Food Processor (optional)


Instructions

1. Dust chicken with flour. Season with salt and pepper.

2. In a large skillet, brown chicken on all sides in oil and butter
over medium-high heat. Remove chicken to platter.

3. In the same skillet, cook and stir the onion, celery, pepper and mushrooms for 5 minutes.
Stir in the tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, wine, herbs, garlic and sugar.
Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

4. Return chicken to skillet. Cover and simmer for 45-60 minutes or until chicken is tender.

5. In a large pot, cook pasta of your choice.

6. Plate, serve over pasta, sprinkle with parmesan cheese, and enjoy!

Enjoy!
