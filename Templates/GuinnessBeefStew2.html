Instant Pot Guinness Beef Stew

78 Likes 1 Comment 27th November 2021

This is a classic and hearty one-pot recipe that will feed the family and give leftovers. Goes great with dipping bread! Makes 8 servings.

Check out our automatic serving size adjuster! The recipe can cater to however many guests you are serving. Upon loading, the recipe has a serving size of 8 people.
Ingredients
2.5 Pounds Stew Meat (pre-cut) 2 Onions
4 Carrots 4 Celery Stalks
1 Bay Leaf Rosemary to taste
Thyme to taste 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
4 tbsp Worchestershire Sauce 2 tbsp Minced Garlic
4 Medium Sized Potatoes Parsley to Taste
32oz Guinness Stout 2 Cups Beef Stock
2 tbsp corn starch Salt and Pepper
Kitchen Needs
Instant Pot Large Stirring Spoon
Cutting Board Large Knife


Instructions

1. Turn the Instant Pot on the saute setting. Add a drizzle of olive oil and let it heat up.

2. Add the stew meat. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
Cook beef, stirring occasionally, until just browned. Turn off the instant pot for now. Remove the stew meat and set aside.

3. Slice the onion, carrots and celery.
Add to Instant Pot, turning on the saute funtion agian. Saute for 5 minutes. Turn off the Saute funtion again.

4. Add all other ingredients except for the potatoes. Close the lid and move the valve to sealing. Pressure cook for 30 minutes.

5. Release the pressure and add the potatoes. Pressure cook for another 10 minutes.

6. Release the pressure and mix together the corn starch and 1/4 cup of water. Add the mixture to the stew and stir occassionally until thickened.

Enjoy!
